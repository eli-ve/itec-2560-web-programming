Some of my favorite things

by Elijah

Favorite Movie

My favorite movie is The Fifth Element. It was directed by Luc Besson, co-written by Luc Besson and Robert Mark Kamen, and stars Bruce Willis, Milla Jovovich, and Gary Oldman. Released in 1997, this movie is a sci-fi cult classic. Opinions from critics and viewers alike are polarized, but this movie has been in my top 5 favorites since I first watched it as a child. With stunning costume designs from Jean Paul Gaultier and imaginative alien and set designs, The Fifth Element has been one of my biggest inspirations as an artist. You can learn more about this movie on IMDb.

Favorite Music

My current favorite musician is Dorian Electra. Their debut album Flamboyant is a mix of experimental pop, electropop, baroque pop, and hyperpop. Their music incorporates a variety of LGBTQ themes, with a focus on gender and masculinity. Catchy lyrics and melodies mean that I often get their songs stuck in my head, but I like the music enough that it's not at all irritating. Check out Dorian Electra's website for more information, tour dates, and merch.
